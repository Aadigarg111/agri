How LocaleFi Works: Simple, Transparent, Secure

🤓

1. Form Your Digital Circle

Gather your trusted community – friends, family, or business associates – to create a transparent savings group on Solana.

📱

2. Contribute Effortlessly

Members make regular, agreed-upon contributions directly from their mobile, with transactions secured by the blockchain.

💰

3. Receive Instant Payouts

When it's your turn, the full Circle amount is securely disbursed to your wallet, instantly, with negligible fees.

📌

4. Build On-Chain Trust

Every interaction contributes to your verifiable, decentralized reputation, fostering strong, trustworthy community ties.
